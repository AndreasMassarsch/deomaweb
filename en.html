What we do

Three ways we
create value.

01

Build it ourselves

Our main focus. We identify problems and opportunities in the market and can take it all the way from idea to launch. We build with our own hands and own capital — allowing us to move fast, test early and iterate without asking permission.

02

Join the team

We are also open to joining others' projects where we can contribute both expertise and capital. We only invest in companies where we can actively participate — as operational partners, not passive owners.

03

Advisory

We have built up solid experience over the years and problem-solving is our expertise. We are happy to act as sparring partner, advisor, consultant or board member — always grounded in hands-on experience from building and running real companies.

About us

Deoma in brief.

Deoma — short for Delin & Massarsch — combines curiosity about the latest technology with over 25 years of experience actually driving things through and making them happen. Our core expertise is in web applications, SaaS and AI. We know how to take an idea all the way from whiteboard to market.

We have founded and run companies in e-signing, project management, contract AI and interpretation. Several have been acquired, others we still run. We know what it takes at every stage — from first prototype to profitable growth.

We invest our own capital, work hands-on and stay until it works.
